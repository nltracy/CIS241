Welcome, Traveller.

Hey! Welcome to my website. I'm sure you're a busy person and figuring out everything there is to offer here is a priority. Well sure it is! Quickly find what you need with links to my Resume, Interests, and some personal reflections on sundry topics. They're also listed at the top of your screen for future reference. When you are done looking around, please don't forget to sign the guestbook before you leave.

Humankind is a mass of contradictions. I can claim no differently. A nature lover and admirer of untouched forest, I also enjoy making things from wood. I am a fisherman who refuses to catch anything lest the sting of hook weighs on the piscine minds under troubled waters. Despairing of his fellow human, hope springs forth eternally. As a rabid reader, I haven't words to recall the reduction of my recreational reading recently. Onomatopoeia.
