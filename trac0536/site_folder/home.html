Welcome, Traveller.

Hey! Welcome to my website. I'm sure you're a busy person and figuring out everything there is to offer here is a priority. Well sure it is! Quickly find what you need with links to my Resume, Interests, and some personal reflections on sundry topics. They're also listed at the top of your screen for future reference. When you are done looking around, please don't forget to sign the guestbook before you leave.

Humankind is a mass of contradictions. I can claim no differently. A nature lover and admirer of untouched forest, I also enjoy making things from wood. I am a fisherman who refuses to catch anything lest the sting of hook weighs on those piscine minds lurking under troubled waters. Despairing of his fellow human, hope springs forth eternally. As a rabid reader, I haven't words to recall the reduction of my recreational reading recently. Onomatopoeia.

Blast from the Past:

Graphics Arts Final Project, Spring 2018

Posted November 14, 2018 By Nathan Tracy. Mixed image sources.
From the Text:
"2024 brings the Earth and Mars into alignment for a Low Energy Approach! Compared to our High Energy Approaches, you'll save an average of US $379,000 per-person! Softer engine burns and shorter transit times mean lower physical requirements, so don't forget to bring your grandma too!"

The culmination of a three month process learning Adobe InDesign, this project generated business cards, advertising posters (pictured), and a Style Guide. Space exploration is still a goal of mine, it's just taking a while. The picture on the left is pretty small, but the actual page it comes from is 100MB of thoughtful design.
